Investment Theory

Understanding the relationship between price and volatility is key to successful trading

‚ùå Avoid: High Price, Low Volatility

Expensive items with stable prices offer little profit opportunity. The investment is high but the price rarely moves enough to make meaningful gains.

‚úÖ Target: Medium Price, Medium Volatility

The sweet spot! Items with moderate prices and volatility offer the best balance. You can afford to buy in quantity, prices move enough to profit, and the risk of loss on merchant runs is manageable.

‚ùå Avoid: Low Price, High Volatility

Cheap items with wild price swings might seem tempting, but they rarely add up to real money. The risk of losing items on merchant runs often outweighs the small profit potential.

Updating to a New Version

If you're updating from a previous version, follow these steps to preserve your data:

  1. Close the application if it's currently running
  2. Download the new ZIP file from the link above
  3. Extract the ZIP file to a temporary location
  4. Copy only ABI_Trading_Platform.exe from the extracted folder
  5. Paste it into your existing installation folder, replacing the old executable
  6. Keep your existing trades.json and blacklist.json files - these contain your personal data and should not be overwritten
  7. Launch the application - your data will be preserved automatically
